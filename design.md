# Business

## Overview

This project is so that people will know me and they will know where to find me if they need to contact me.

## Features

Feature 1: This feature is an about me page where people can get to know me as if they have known me their whole life.

Feature 2: This will be the main page that the user can look to for info on what this page is about so that they can learn what this was created for.'

Feature 3: I will also show a page where you can take quizzes after reading the About Me page to test how much you know on me. If you answer all the questions correctly, you get a prize!

## Goals

Short term goal: so that my friends and family can get to know more about me.

Long Term goal: My long term goal is that a lot of different people who may have never met me would know about me and what i like.

## Technical Design

It is a static website that will show my interests and hobbies to people who may have never met me. 


### Architecture

The architecture of this project is going to be pretty simple. It is going to consist of 3 pages that will each feature different things.
